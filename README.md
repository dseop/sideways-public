## 📸 About Sideways
**Sideways** solves a common ergonomic problem: the discomfort of rotating your phone for landscape shots. It allows you to capture perfect 16:9 or 4:3 horizontal photos while maintaining a natural, secure vertical (portrait) grip.

Ideal for one-handed shooting, travel, and capturing wide vistas without the wrist strain.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Sideways Mode:** Capture landscape photos from a portrait orientation. A real-time 16:9 guide helps you frame the perfect shot.
- **Full Mode:** Access the full sensor for standard high-quality captures.
- **Resolution Control:** Toggle between `High Res` (Native) for maximum detail and `Low Res` (1080p) for efficiency.
- **In-App Gallery:** Instantly preview and manage your captured shots.
- **Privacy First:** All image processing is done locally. Your photos stay on your device.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **SwiftUI:** For a modern, responsive user interface.
- **AVFoundation:** High-performance camera session management and real-time image processing.
- **PhotoKit:** Seamless integration with the iOS Photos library.
